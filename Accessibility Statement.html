Accessibility Statement for Agile Acccounting

This is an accessibility statement from Agile Acccounting.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Agile Acccounting is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Additional accessibility considerations

This site has been designed and developed to ensure that its content is available to as many users as possible, including:

  1. people with disabilities who may use assistive technologies
  2. people with slower internet connections (including some rural and regional users) or less than state-of-the-art equipment
  3. people with non-PC internet devices including handheld devices and mobile phones.

The accessibility features available on this site include:

  1. access keys to access certain information using your keyboard
  2. skip links and a logical tab order to navigate using assistive technologies
  3. adherence to international web standards
  4. ARIA compliant keyboard navigation for rich interactive components

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Agile Accounting. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on Agile Accounting:

  1. Phone: +1 (408) 214-9890
  2. E-mail: info@agileac.com

We try to respond to feedback within 10 business days.
